## üîÑ Regenerating Results

Results are automatically regenerated by the [GitHub Actions workflow](https://github.com/InquiryInstitute/diophantine/actions). To regenerate manually:

1. Open the [Colab notebook](https://colab.research.google.com/github/InquiryInstitute/diophantine/blob/main/diophantine_exploration.ipynb)
2. Run all cells
3. Results will be saved to `results/` and `figures/` directories

Or trigger the workflow:
- Go to [Actions](https://github.com/InquiryInstitute/diophantine/actions)
- Select "Run Analysis and LLM Review"
- Click "Run workflow"
